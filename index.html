About Me

A Dev Who Builds & Secures

I'm a recent B.Tech graduate in Software Development from the University of Buea, Cameroon. My core strength is Flutter & Firebase mobile development, and I'm actively expanding into cybersecurity — running a Kali Linux home lab, practicing with nmap, and working through TryHackMe structured learning paths.

My flagship project Agrinexa is an agriculture-focused mobile app for Cameroon with Google Auth, real-time Firestore, and MTN MoMo / Orange Money payments via CinetPay. I care about building things that make a real difference in African tech.
